    Founded in August 2020 by Kevin Tan, THE.LAB.701 is more than just a coffee shop - it's a unique cozy space where "Exploring, Creating, and Sharing" experiences from around the world come together. Born from Kevin's apartment's storage room (701), this space has evolved into a vibrant hub for building positive energy and community connections.
